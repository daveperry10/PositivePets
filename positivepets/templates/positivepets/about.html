About Positive Pets

Positive Pets is a place to share information about pets. Kids will enjoy:
  • writing about their pets in their own editable space
  • reading about their friends' pets
  • communicating with each other through basic chatroom and closed-system email
  • having fun with picture uploads (coming soon: video and audio files)
Positive Pets is a safe environment for learning about the technology adults are using.
  • No data collected
  • No ads
  • No strangers
  • No video games
  • Special parent user status offers full view of messaging activity
Positive Pets is easy
  • Names are not case-sensitive
  • Simple passwords allowed
  • Choice-lists for email recipient selection
