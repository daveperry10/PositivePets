About Positive Pets
Positive Pets is a place to share information about pets. Kids will enjoy:
  • Writing about their pets and themselves
  • Reading about their friends and their friends' pets
  • Emailing and chatting
  • Customizing their profile page
Positive Pets is a safe environment for learning about the technology adults are using.
  • No personal details stored
  • No tracking, data collection or ads
  • No strangers
Positive Pets is easy
  • Names and passwords are not case-sensitive
  • Simple passwords are allowed
  • No free text requirement -- all operations are driven by choice-lists
